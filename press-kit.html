About

HSA Monster is an iOS, iPadOS, and macOS app that helps families turn their Health Savings Account into a powerful retirement tool. Snap receipts with on-device AI, track every tax-free dollar you can reimburse later, and sync privately across Apple devices via iCloud.

Key Features

  • Capture receipts in seconds with on-device AI — no typing, no data entry.
  • Always know your tax-free reimbursable balance.
  • Built for families — both spouses can add receipts and sync via iCloud.
  • Export annual statements and full receipt archives in one tap.
  • Universal Apple app for iPhone, iPad, and Mac.
  • Private by design: all AI runs on-device; data lives in your personal iCloud.
  • No accounts, no logins, no ads.
  • Family Plan covers up to 6 family members via Apple Family Sharing.
